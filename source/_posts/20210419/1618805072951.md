April 19 news, the Los Angeles Lakers superstar LeBron James is in the stage of injury recovery, "Fox Sports" mouthpiece, famous janhei skip Bayless recently turned the target to the Golden State Warriors star Stephen curry, frankly said curry is just the greatest regular season shooter.

In the last game against Celtic, Stephen curry scored 47 points in 15 of 27 shots, including 11 three-point goals. This is curry's fifth single game hit 10 + three-point goal in this season. Curry has scored 44 three-point goals in the past five games, setting a record of three-point hits in five league games. It is worth mentioning that curry has scored 30 + in 10 consecutive games, averaging 39.1 points, 6.4 rebounds, 4.5 assists and 54.6% shooting percentage, leading the warriors to 6-4.

For skip Bayless, however, curry is just a great regular season shooter. "In Boston, curry proved once again that he was the greatest regular season shooter in history, just in the regular season. ¡±Skip Bayless commented.

So far in his career, curry has led the warriors to seven playoffs, five finals and three championships, with an average contribution of 26.5 points.
