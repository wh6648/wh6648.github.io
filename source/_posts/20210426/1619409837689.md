April 26 news, NBA regular season continues. The home team beat the Suns 128-119 in four quarters and won two games in a row to continue to lead the East. After the game, Nets coach Steve Nash praised Kevin Durant and Carey Owen.

Owen scored 16 points in the first quarter, with 34 points, 6 rebounds and 12 assists, while KD scored 33 points, 6 rebounds and 4 assists. Two super giants of the nets got 67 points and 16 assists in 22 of 40 shots. After the game, Nash said: "this is a very typical Owen + Durant game."

It's the Nets' 20th win (20-6) against the west this season, and 18 of the last 21 against the West. 20 wins against the western team in a single season, setting a new record in the history of the nets.

KD played for 28 minutes. Nash said before the game that he would limit KD's playing time. "In the next 2-3 games, his playing time will be less than 30 minutes," Nash said, "and I don't want him to play back-to-back."

After today's game, Nash's view did not change, "limiting KD's playing time is what we always insist on."

Speaking of today's tactics against the Suns, Nash said: "to be honest, I hope the team can shoot more three points, but the problem is that we have a lot of very good midfield shooters."
