May 9 news, NBA officials yesterday announced the latest MVP rankings, Nuggets star Nicolas Jokic ranked first for the sixth week in a row, 76ers star Joel nbid ranked second. Today, enbid talked about MVP in an interview.

"I'm a little tired of pursuing these awards. I want to win a bigger honor - the championship. We have a very good chance now, we are a very good team He said.

"It's a great achievement for our team. If he (nbid) wins the MVP, it will be great. It means we are a successful team," said 76ers coach Doug rivers

In the game against the rockets on May 6, enbid scored 34 points and 12 rebounds in just 24 minutes, his 15th 30 + 10 game of the season. Since May, nbid has contributed two doubles per game. The MVP fight between him and Jokic has attracted the attention of hall of Fame center olajuwan. Olajuwan believes that it is time for those players who dominate the interior to win the award again.

It is worth mentioning that enbid has come forward to speak for himself before, publicly saying that he should be the undoubted MVP¡° Of course I should get the MVP because I've dominated the stadium all season and I won't stop He said.
