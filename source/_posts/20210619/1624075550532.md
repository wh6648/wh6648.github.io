According to reports from the US media on June 19, the Wizards guard Bradley bill and Suns point guard Devon Booker promised to represent the US men's basketball team in the Tokyo Olympic Games.

According to a tip from the famous journalist of the Olympic, Samas chalaniya, bill has made a commitment to the U.S. men's basketball team. According to previous rumors, the U.S. team also hopes bill can put on the national team robe.

ESPN well-known reporter Adrian wernalovski follow-up report, he heard that Booker has also made a commitment, he will go to Tokyo Olympic Games with the team.

Booker is currently preparing for the Western Conference finals. Booker's suns beat the Nuggets 4-0 in the Western Conference semi-final, the first time in Booker's career.

Bill also took part in this year's playoffs. His team, the wizards, was eliminated by the 76ers 4-1 in the first round of the playoffs.

So far, five NBA stars have committed to participate in the Tokyo Olympic Games. In addition to bill and Booker, the other three are Damian lilard, Jason Tatum and Drummond green.

LeBron James and Anthony Davis of the Los Angeles Lakers will not travel to Tokyo due to injuries, while warriors superstar Stephen curry is still weighing whether to play.
