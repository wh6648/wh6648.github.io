On May 24, the 2020-21 NBA playoffs continued, with the Phoenix Suns playing the Los Angeles Lakers at home. In the end, the Suns beat the Lakers 99-90 and made a good start to the series.

Single quarter score: 25-32, 20-21, 23-28, 22-18

In terms of data, on the Laker side, James has 18 points, 7 rebounds, 10 assists, Schroeder has 14 points, Anthony Davis has 13 points, 7 rebounds, Drummond has 12 points, 9 rebounds, Harrell has 12 points, and Caruso has 10 points. On the Suns side, Booker has 34 points, 7 rebounds and 8 assists, Eaton has 21 points, 16 rebounds, bridges has 10 points and Johnson has 10 points and 6 rebounds.

After the start of the game, the offensive efficiency of the two teams was not high. Then, Schroeder made a success near the free throw line to help the Lakers open the first record. Then, Booker received Paul's pass and scored three points in response. After that, the two teams cut points by attacking the interior line one after another, and the score rose alternately. By the middle of the quarter, James had three points in a row to help the Lakers get a five point lead. Subsequently, Aton and Paul cut points in a row to help the Suns directly equalise the score. In the second half of this season, the Lakers suddenly lost their temper, and the Suns took the opportunity to cut points continuously, playing a small wave of 9-0 climax, gaining an 8-point lead. After that, although the Lakers struggled to score, the Suns responded repeatedly. At the end of the quarter, the Suns led the Lakers 32-25.

After the start of the second quarter, the two teams cut points continuously by attacking the interior. Then, Saric cut points one after another to help the Suns get a double-digit lead. After that, Paul was injured and sent directly to the dressing room for examination. The Lakers then used Harrell's continuous scoring to narrow the gap to three points. After that, Booker broke through and buckled his hands to help the Suns stabilize the situation. After that, the two teams fell into a tug of war situation again, and the score rose alternately. In the second half of this section, Booker cut points continuously to help the Suns get a double-digit lead again. At the end of the quarter, the Suns took a temporary 53-45 lead over the Lakers.

After the third quarter of the game, the two teams began to attack each other, and the score rose alternately. Although the Lakers struggled to catch up with the points, Booker and Paul cut points one after another to help the Suns keep a 10 point lead. In the middle of this section, Claude and bridges cut points continuously to help the Suns expand the difference to 15 points and stop the Lakers directly. After the time-out, the Los Angeles team blossomed more. After Schroeder got the layup, the Los Angeles team reduced the difference to 7 points, and also stopped the Suns directly. After that, Penn scored three points to help the Suns stop bleeding. After that, Booker scored a series of free throws. At the end of the quarter, the Suns led the Lakers 81-68.

After the start of the fourth quarter, Booker continued to cut points to help the Suns expand the difference to 16 points. After that, the Lakers continued to chase points, reducing the difference to nearly 10 points. Then, there was a sudden conflict on the field, James collided with Paul, James fell to the ground. Then, Caruso clashed with Penn, and Harrell rushed forward and directly knocked Penn away. Penn was directly expelled from the field for hitting Caruso with the ball, and both Caruso and Harrell also suffered from technical fouls. After that, under the leadership of Paul and Booker, the Suns kept the difference around 10 points. In the end, the Suns beat the Lakers 99-90 and made a good start to the series£¨ Peas)
